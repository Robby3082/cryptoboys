Questions and Answers

If you have any questions feel free to contact us on our discord

How many Crypto Boys exist?

Crypto Boys is a collection of 888 uniquely generated NFTs which will be stored forever on the Solana Blockchain.

What is the price of a Crypto Boy?

Each Crypto Boy can be minted for 0.88 SOL.

Will Crypto Boys be listed on secondary?

Yes, directly after mint Crypto Boys will be listed on Magic Eden.

Which wallet should I use to mint?

Please use the Phantom Wallet to mint. To add the wallet extension to Chrome, follow these steps:
Phantom App: https://phantom.app/
Add the phantom wallet extension to chrome.
Create a wallet. Never share your seed phrase with anyone!
Transfer SOL to your wallet from the exchange of your choice. ex. (binance, coinbase, crypto.com)
Connect your wallet, click mint, and approve the transaction.
The CryptoBoy will now be in your wallet :)
